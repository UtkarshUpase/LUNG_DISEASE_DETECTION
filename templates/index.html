Lung Cancer

Lung cancer, a leading global cause of cancer mortality, arises from uncontrolled cell growth in lung tissue. Symptoms include persistent cough, hemoptysis, dyspnea, chest pain, and weight loss. Risk factors include smoking, asbestos exposure, and family history. Our AI-powered detection system analyzes histopathological images to identify adenocarcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, or benign tissue with 97% accuracy. Trained on over 15,000 images, it detects subtle patterns like irregular nodules or spiculated masses, enabling early intervention. Upload your screening reports to leverage our cutting-edge technology for precise diagnosis and timely treatment planning.

Tuberculosis

Tuberculosis (TB), caused by Mycobacterium tuberculosis, is a major infectious disease affecting the lungs. Symptoms include chronic cough, night sweats, fever, weight loss, and hemoptysis. Risk factors include HIV, crowded living conditions, and immunocompromise. Our AI system, trained on 12,000 chest X-rays and CT scans, detects TB with 94% accuracy, identifying cavitary lesions, miliary nodules, and tree-in-bud patterns. This enables rapid diagnosis in high-risk populations. Submit your TB screening reports to our platform for precise analysis and early treatment, reducing the spread and severity of this curable disease.

Pneumonia

Pneumonia, an infection of the lung’s alveoli, is caused by bacteria, viruses, or fungi, leading to cough, fever, dyspnea, and chest pain. Risk factors include chronic lung disease, immunocompromise, and smoking. Our AI model, trained on 10,000 CT scans, achieves 94% accuracy in detecting consolidation, ground-glass opacities, and air bronchograms, distinguishing bacterial from viral pneumonia. This aids in selecting appropriate treatments. Upload your diagnostic reports to our platform for fast, accurate analysis, enabling timely intervention to manage this potentially life-threatening condition effectively.
