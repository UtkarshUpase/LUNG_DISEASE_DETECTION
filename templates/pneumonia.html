About This Model

Our pneumonia detection model uses a deep convolutional neural network trained on over 10,000 CT scan images. It can identify early signs of pneumonia with an accuracy of 94%.

The model analyzes patterns in lung tissue and can detect nodules as small as 3mm in diameter, which are often missed in regular screenings.
